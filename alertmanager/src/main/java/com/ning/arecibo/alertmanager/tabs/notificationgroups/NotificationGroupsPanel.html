Here you can manage Notification Groups, for creating groups of recipients for alert emails.

Notification Groups Explained
Explanation

A Notification Group is a way for grouping multiple recipients to receive a the same alert notification. A Notification Group can be associated with one or more Alerting Configurations. A Notification Group must include at least one email contact, which can be for an individual person or an email alias. It may be necessary to set up a Person or Alias before creating a Threshold Definition.

To associate a Notification Group with an Alerting Configuration, visit the Alerting Configurations tab.
To manage Email Contacts, visit the People & Aliases tab.
