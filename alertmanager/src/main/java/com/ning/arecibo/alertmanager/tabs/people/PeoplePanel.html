People & Aliases Explained  |  Notification Types Explained
Explanation

There are 2 types of contacts that can receive emails, either an individual Person or an Email Group Alias. We want to know if a contact is a Person or not, since it will be used as part of the Alert Acknowledgement system. Only Persons will be allowed to acknowledge alerts. A Person or Alias can be associated with a Notification Group.

To associate a Person or alias with a Notification Group, visit the Notification Groups tab.

There are 2 types of Notification Type, either by REGULAR_EMAIL or else by SMS_VIA_EMAIL. The difference, is that for the SMS_VIA_EMAIL, a reduced version of the notification email will be sent, limited to 140 characters, to conform to the limitation of SMS text messaging systems.
